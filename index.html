About Elon Musk

Elon Reeve Musk (born June 28, 1971) is a business magnate, industrial designer and engineer. He is the founder, CEO, CTO and chief designer of SpaceX; early investor, CEO and product architect of Tesla, Inc.; founder of The Boring Company; co-founder of Neuralink; and co-founder and initial co-chairman of OpenAI. A centibillionaire, Musk became the richest person in the world in January 2021, surpassing Jeff Bezos.





Here are some of Elon Musk's accomplishments:

1. He sold his first company for a whopping 307 million dollars!

At just 27, Elon and his brother sold their first company, Zip2. The sale made Elon 22 million dollars richer!

2. He cofounded PayPal

Elon created a website called x.com (an internet finance company). After competing against another interent finance company called Cofinity he decided to buy out and merge with them. The merger allowed Elon to invest in and build up PayPal. Two years later, he sold PayPal to eBay for a cool 1.5 billion dollars. He gained 180 million dollars off that transaction!

3. He created the Musk foundation

Elon and his brother created a private foundation to support four causes: Renewable energy research and advocacy, human space exploration, science and engineering education, and pediatric research. He has donated more than 300 million dollars to date.
